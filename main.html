Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a classic film directed by Frank Darabont. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongly convicted of murder and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Through perseverance and friendship, Andy finds a way to bring hope and redemption to his fellow inmates.

Actors

The movie features exceptional performances by actors like Tim Robbins (Andy Dufresne) and Morgan Freeman (Ellis "Red" Redding).
